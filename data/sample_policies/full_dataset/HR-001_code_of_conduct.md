## 2. PURPOSE

The purpose of this **Code of Conduct** is to establish clear, consistent behavioral and ethical standards for all personnel employed by **Innovatech Solutions Inc.** This policy ensures that our business operations are conducted with the highest level of integrity, professionalism, and respect.

This policy serves to protect Innovatech Solutions’ reputation, assets, and compliance standing across all business activities. Adherence to these standards is fundamental to maintaining the trust of our customers, partners, shareholders, and fellow employees.

These standards directly support Innovatech Solutions’ core values: **Integrity First, Customer Focus, and Collaborative Excellence.** Every employee is expected to embody these values in their daily interactions and decision-making processes.

---

## 3. SCOPE

This Code of Conduct applies to **all individuals** working for Innovatech Solutions Inc., including:

*   Full-time, part-time, and temporary employees.
*   Contractors, consultants, and vendors performing services on behalf of the company.
*   Members of the Board of Directors, where applicable.

This policy covers conduct occurring on company premises, at company-sponsored events, while using company-owned or third-party systems (including remote work setups), and any external conduct that negatively impacts the company’s business interests or reputation.

**Exclusions:** No formal exclusions exist for this mandatory policy; however, specific disciplinary actions outlined in Section 8 will be tailored based on employment status (employee vs. contractor).

## 4. DEFINITIONS

| Term | Definition |

| **Whistleblower** | An individual who reports suspected illegal, unethical, or improper practices within the organization to management or external authorities. |

## 5. POLICY STATEMENTS

The following statements outline the mandatory standards of conduct required of all personnel:

### 5.1 Employee Conduct Expectations

*   Employees **must** treat all colleagues, customers, and partners with dignity and respect, fostering an inclusive environment free from intimidation or bias.
*   Employees **must not** engage in any form of harassment, bullying, or discrimination based on any **Protected Characteristic**.

### 5.2 Equal Opportunity and Anti-Discrimination

*   Innovatech Solutions is an **Equal Opportunity Employer**. Employment decisions **shall** be made without regard to Protected Characteristics.

### 5.3 Confidentiality and Data Security

*   Employees **must** protect all **Confidential Information (CI)** in accordance with the Data Security Policy (HR-005).
*   Employees **shall not** disclose proprietary source code or unreleased financial metrics outside the company, except as required by a signed Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) or legal subpoena.
*   Employees **must** report any actual or suspected data breach involving customer or company data to the IT Security Department within **two (2) hours** of discovery.

### 5.4 Conflicts of Interest

*   Employees **are required to** disclose any potential or actual **Conflict of Interest (COI)** to their direct Manager and the Compliance Officer in writing within **seven (7) calendar days** of the conflict arising.
*   Employees **must not** accept gifts, entertainment, or gratuities from suppliers or customers valued in excess of **$150 USD** per item, unless pre-approved by a Director-level executive.

### 5.5 Workplace Safety and Substance Abuse

*   Employees **must not** report to work or operate Company equipment while under the influence of illegal drugs or alcohol.
*   Employees **must** immediately report any unsafe working condition or workplace injury to their Manager and the Facilities department, referencing the Workplace Safety Protocol (OHS-002).

### 5.6 Performance and Compliance Reporting

*   Performance reviews **will** be conducted annually, based on documented performance metrics and adherence to all company policies, including this Code of Conduct.
*   Employees **should** utilize internal channels to report ethical concerns; however, employees **are permitted** to report suspected violations of law to appropriate external regulatory bodies without fear of retaliation (Whistleblower Protection).

### 5.7 Financial Integrity

*   All expense reports **must** comply with the Travel and Expense Policy (FIN-003). Any expense report found to contain false claims exceeding **$50 USD** will result in disciplinary action.
*   Employees **must not** engage in **Insider Trading** involving Innovatech Solutions stock or the stock of any partner company for which they have CI access.

## 6. PROCEDURES

### 6.1 Procedure for Reporting Misconduct

1.1. **Identify the Concern:** The reporting party identifies behavior that violates this Code of Conduct (e.g., discrimination, fraud, theft of company time).

1.2. **Initial Reporting:** The party **should** first attempt to resolve the issue informally with their direct Manager, unless the Manager is the subject of the complaint.

1.3. **Formal Escalation:** If informal resolution fails, or if the issue involves senior management, the concern **must** be reported immediately to the **HR Department** or via the confidential, third-party Ethics Hotline (available 24/7).

1.4. **Documentation:** All formal reports must include the date, time, location, individuals involved, and a factual description of the alleged violation.

### 6.2 Procedure for Conflict of Interest Disclosure and Review

2.1. **Disclosure:** The employee completes the **COI Disclosure Form (Appendix A)** and submits it to their Manager.

2.2. **Manager Review:** The Manager reviews the disclosure and forwards the form, with their recommendation, to the **Compliance Officer** within **five (5) business days**.

2.3. **Final Determination:** The Compliance Officer, in consultation with the CHRO, will issue a written determination within **ten (10) business days**, either approving the activity, requiring mitigation steps, or prohibiting the activity.

2.4. **Record Keeping:** All COI reviews and determinations **shall** be archived by the Compliance Department for a minimum of **seven (7) years**.

## 8. COMPLIANCE AND ENFORCEMENT

**Monitoring:** Compliance with this Code of Conduct will be monitored through mandatory annual training completion records, internal audit procedures (conducted quarterly by the Internal Audit team), and regular review of expense reports and access logs.

**Reporting Violations:** All employees are obligated to report suspected violations promptly, as outlined in Section 6.1. Innovatech Solutions strictly prohibits retaliation against any employee who makes a good-faith report of a suspected violation.

**Consequences of Violations:** Violations of this Code of Conduct will result in disciplinary action up to and including immediate termination of employment or contract, depending on the severity of the breach. Serious violations, especially those involving illegal activity, fraud, or gross safety negligence, may be referred to law enforcement.

---

## 9. EXCEPTIONS

Any request for a deviation or exception to a mandatory requirement within this Code of Conduct **must** be submitted in writing to the **CHRO**.

Exceptions are granted only in extraordinary circumstances where adherence to the policy creates an undue hardship that outweighs the risk to the Company, and where alternative safeguards can be put in place. All exceptions **must** be approved in writing by both the **CHRO** and the **CEO** and documented in the Policy Exceptions Log. Exceptions will be reviewed annually.
